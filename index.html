About Me


I'm currently a researcher at INESC TEC and recently finished the Master in Informatics and Computing Engineering at FEUP.

My main skills are web and mobile development. I have multiple projects in this area.

I also have projects in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Information Systems, Computer graphics, Computer Networks and Data Mining.

My main interests focus on web and mobile development, data bases and data mining.

My Master thesis - Named entity extraction from Portuguese web text - was in the Information Extraction field, where its main purpose was to find a way to extract entities from Portuguese texts.

Apart from informatics, I enjoy playing the piano.
